CPBS

Introduction

Is a monitor and regulate system implemented by the Harare City Council and Traffic Safety Council of Zimbabwe to mitigate traffic congestion and road accidents. The system works by allowing drivers to book for parking spots in designated car parks around the CBD of Harare.


Usage

The system has been implemented by splitting the CBD into 5 zones each with its allocated car park.

The driver is able to book for a car parking spot in a designated zone depending on the zone they which to travel to.
